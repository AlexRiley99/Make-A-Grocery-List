Make A Grocery List!

❓

Instructions:

  1. Enter your budget amount and press "Set Budget". This number will display under "Your List"
  2. Indicate whether you'd like to be alerted if you go over budget. If yes, you'll be given an alert if you enter an item that would put you over your allotted budget amount.
  3. Enter the name of an item.
  4. Enter how many you'd like to buy. Items such as a bunch of bananas or multi-pack of juice boxes count as one item
  5. Enter the anticipated cost. Please enter the cost of only one item, even if you plan on buying multiple.
  6. Press the "Add" button and your list items will appear under "Your List".
  7. Repeat steps 3-6 until your list is complete.
  8. Press "Done" to see your total and the difference between your total and your budget. If you press "Done" before you press "Add" on your final item, just press "Add" one last time and then press "Done" to re-calculate all values.
